About Us

Mombasa Climate Space is a community-based organization dedicated to advancing climate resilience, environmental conservation, community empowerment and sustainable development across Mombasa County.


We collaborate with communities, schools, youth groups, NGOs, government institutions and development partners to build a greener and more sustainable future.
