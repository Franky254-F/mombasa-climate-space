About Us

Mombasa Climate Space is a community-based organization dedicated to advancing climate resilience, environmental conservation, community empowerment and sustainable development across Mombasa County.


We collaborate with communities, schools, youth groups, government institutions and development partners to build a greener and more sustainable future.

Our Focus Areas

Climate Action

Promoting community-led climate adaptation and mitigation solutions.

Tree Planting

Restoring ecosystems through afforestation initiatives.

Youth Empowerment

Engaging young people in environmental leadership.

Environmental Education

Creating awareness on sustainability and conservation.
